Alpine.js Weekly - Thank You

Thanks for signing up to the Alpine.js Weekly newsletter, it’s great to have you!

Just a few steps left to make sure you get the best out of it.

Confirm your email address

  1. Open your email account
  2. Open the email with title “Confirm your subscription to Alpine.js Weekly”
  3. Click the “Click here to confirm” link

Make sure you receive new issues

You’ll shortly receive the latest issue the Alpine.js Weekly newsletter, follow these few steps to make sure you receive the emails:

  1. Mark the domain as “Not Spam” or trusted (this will usually happen if you’re using Gmail + a custom domain)
  2. For Gmail users, mark as not “Promotions”
  3. Add the newsletter address to your contacts: hugo@alpinejs-news.codewithhugo.com

The newsletter goes out once a week on Friday.
